Overview

This project builds a Neural Radiance Field (NeRF) from my own image capture. The pipeline follows four stages: (0) camera calibration and pose estimation with ArUco tags; (1) NeRF training on the undistorted images and poses; (2) quality improvements and diagnostics; and (3) visualizations and novel-view renderings.

What I implement in Part 0
Tag-based calibration (OpenCV ArUco), robust PnP for per-frame camera poses, undistortion with an optimal new intrinsics (cropped ROI, corrected principal point), and packaging images/poses/focal into a compact .npz dataset for training.
