Overview

In this part, we implement planar homographies to warp images into new perspectives and stitch them into mosaics. The pipeline covers: selecting correspondences, computing a least‑squares homography via DLT, warping with inverse mapping, and blending overlapped regions. Results include rectification of planar surfaces and panoramic mosaics.

What you’ll see: point selection UI screenshots, homography math, rectified results, pairwise stitching, and final mosaics.
